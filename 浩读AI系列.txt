Haodu Edge‑TTS – AI‑Powered Voice‑Reading Suite
Haodu Edge‑TTS is an AI‑enhanced learning and teaching assistant. It embeds a flexible AI engine that allows users to add custom models and tailor AI‑driven instruction. The voice‑reading function relies on Microsoft’s neural‑network natural‑speech service (Edge‑TTS) and comes with a global library of nearly 600 mainstream voices. The program offers a full range of tools for both students and teachers, including word management, text‑to‑speech, translation, listening drills, audio‑file processing, and memorization‑verification.
For Students
Haodu helps learners work through an entire lesson from start to finish. Its capabilities include:
Word Management
Analyses a text, generates a word list, automatically identifies new vocabulary, and supports word‑testing and dictation exercises.
Text‑to‑Speech
Reads the whole text, a selected paragraph, a single sentence, user‑chosen content, or from the current cursor position.
Translation
Offers an offline dictionary of roughly 70 000 entries plus access to authoritative online dictionaries.
Users can highlight a word for instant AI‑powered definition and usage, or highlight a sentence/paragraph for full‑sentence translation.
Listening Training
Provides full‑text listening, paragraph‑by‑paragraph, sentence‑by‑sentence, word‑by‑word dictation, and other listening‑practice modes.
Audio‑File Generation
Quickly creates bilingual (Chinese‑English) audio files for the whole text, selected paragraphs, sentences, or word lists.
The resulting MP3 files can be played on phones or any MP3 player while displaying the original script.
Memorization & Full‑Text Check
Plays the translated version sentence by sentence; the student then translates each sentence back into the original language, facilitating memorization of the source text.
For Teachers
Haodu assists educators in preparing and delivering language lessons:
Audio‑Enhanced PPTs
Enables teachers to add spoken narration to every slide, turning a standard PowerPoint into a fully voiced presentation.
Word, Sentence, Dialogue & Text Reading
Supports teachers in providing spoken models for isolated words, sentences, dialogues, and entire passages, with optional “read‑along” mode.
Classroom Dictation
Facilitates on‑the‑spot dictation activities, allowing teachers to generate audio prompts instantly.
Listening Assessment
Provides tools for pre‑class listening checks, module‑level, unit‑level, mid‑term, and final listening tests.
Word‑Usage Analysis
Analyzes the vocabulary used in a text, reports frequencies, identifies typical usages, and gathers example sentences for classroom discussion.
Timed Oral‑Response Tests
Allows teachers to create limited‑time speaking drills that train students’ listening comprehension and oral expression simultaneously.
Custom Listening Library
Enables teachers to build and manage a personal listening corpus, which can be added to a “voice lab” for extensive (broad‑listening) or focused (intensive‑listening) practice.
In summary, Haodu Edge‑TTS combines a powerful AI engine, an extensive multilingual voice library, and a suite of pedagogical tools to make language acquisition more efficient and engaging for both learners and instructors.

学生：

课文朗读：软件可以进行全文朗读、逐段朗读、逐句朗读、选择内容朗读和从光标处朗读等。
课文翻译：软件提供本地近 7 万词库的字典及网络权威字典，划词即可用AI查到单词意思及用法，划句、段可以翻译出意思。
听力训练：软件可以进行通篇听读、单段、单句、单词听写和听力练习等。
声音文件处理：软件可以快速制作原文、单段、单句、单词表中英对照声音文件，声音文件在手机或 MP3 播放机可以播放并显示原文。
课文背诵及全文检测：软件可以利用课文翻译文进行逐句播放，学生进行逐句中英翻译，进而背诵原文。

教师：
对于教师来说，浩读可以辅助他们完成以下工作：
制作有声的 PPT：软件可以辅助教师制作有声的 PPT，给所有的文字配上语音。
单词、句子、对话和课文朗读：软件可以辅助教师完成单词、句子、对话和课文朗读及带读。
课堂听写：软件可以辅助教师完成课堂听写。
听力检测：软件可以辅助教师完成预备听力课堂检测、模块听力检测、单元听力检测、期中听力检测和期末听力检测。
单词分析：软件可以辅助教师分析课文所用的单词量、单词用法和单词例句收集。
制作限定性口语限时回答测试：软件可以辅助教师制作限定性口语限时回答测试训练来提高学生听力及口语表达。
